How Text Generator Works

First, get a link from the repository and fetch the page from the web. Then extract the links from the page and add them to the repository for future processing. Finally, extract the text from the page, converting all alpha characters to lower case and all non-alpha characters to spaces. This creates a total of 27 characters. (26 alphabet + 1 space)

Next, process the extracted text. Build 3 catalogs: one for single-character sequences, one for four-character sequences and one for eight-character sequences.

The potential size of the catalogs grows exponentially, so the single-character catalog has only 27 entries, whereas the eight-character catalog has the potential to grow to over 282 billion entries.

In order to generate text from an N-character catalog, a starting sequence is chosen from the catalog based on the weight (number of occurences) of the sequence. Then each subsequent selection from the catalog is based on the trailing N-1 characters of the previously selected sequence.
